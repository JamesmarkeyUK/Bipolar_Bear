🔒 Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

Your mental health data is personal. Bipolar Bear is built on the principle that your data belongs to you. We do not sell it, share it with advertisers, or use it for any purpose other than making the app work for you.

1. Who we are

Bipolar Bear is an independent app developed to help people living with bipolar disorder track their mood, sleep, energy and wellbeing. The app is available on iOS, Android and the web at bipolarbear.app.

For privacy enquiries, contact: hello@bipolarbear.app

2. What data we collect

Journal data (stored on your device and optionally synced)

  • Mood, energy level, sleep hours
  • Medication taken/not taken
  • Daily goals, notes
  • Optional additional data you choose to track: anxiety, stress, irritability, exercise, time outside, alcohol, smoking, and/or drug use
  • Step count and sleep data (read from Apple Health on iOS — only if you grant permission)

Account data (only if you create an account)

  • Email address and password (used only to identify your account for cloud sync)

Personal details (optional, stored locally and synced if logged in)

  • Name, diagnosis, medications, and any notes you choose to enter in the Personal Details section

Anonymous usage

  • When you tap "I needed this today" we record an anonymous count using Firebase. No personal data is stored for this action.

3. How your data is stored

By default, all journal data is stored locally on your device using your browser's local storage. It never leaves your device unless you choose to create an account and enable cloud sync.

If you create an account, your data is synced to Google Firebase Firestore, a secure cloud database. Data is stored under your unique user ID and is not accessible to other users.

Firebase is a Google service. You can review Google's data practices at firebase.google.com/support/privacy.

4. Health data (Apple Health / HealthKit)

On iOS, Bipolar Bear can optionally read your step count and sleep data from Apple Health to auto-fill your journal entries. This requires your explicit permission.

  • We only read health data — we never write to Apple Health
  • Health data is used solely to populate your journal and is not sent anywhere else
  • You can revoke this permission at any time in iOS Settings → Privacy → Health

5. Notifications

If you enable daily reminders, notification preferences are stored locally on your device. We do not use a third-party push notification service — reminders are scheduled entirely on-device.

6. Who we share your data with

We do not sell, rent or share your personal data with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes. The only third-party service that may receive your data is:

  • Google Firebase — for cloud sync and authentication, if you create an account

We do not use analytics services, advertising networks or any other third-party tracking.

7. Your rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data — use the "Backup data" export in the app
  • Delete your data — clear your local data from device settings, or contact us to delete your cloud account and all associated data
  • Portability — export your data as JSON at any time from within the app

To request deletion of your account and cloud data, email hello@bipolarbear.app from the address associated with your account.

8. Data retention

Local data remains on your device until you delete it or uninstall the app. Cloud data is retained until you delete your account. We do not set automatic expiry on your journal entries.

9. Children

Bipolar Bear is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

10. Sensitive data

Mental health and health information is considered sensitive personal data under GDPR. We take this seriously. Your journal data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS) and at rest within Firebase. We access your data only when necessary to provide technical support, and only with your consent.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will update the "last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

12. Contact

For any privacy questions or data requests: hello@bipolarbear.app
