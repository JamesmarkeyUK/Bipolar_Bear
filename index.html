How your data stays safe

🔑 Your PIN locks the app locally on your device. Without it, nobody can open Bipolar Bear — even if they have your phone.

🔒 Your password encrypts your journal entries before they ever leave your device. The data is scrambled using a key only you hold.

☁️ Firebase (our database) stores only the encrypted version. Even we can't read your entries — they're meaningless without your password.

🛡️ End-to-end encryption means your data is protected at every step — on your device, in transit, and in storage.

🔐 Why is there a PIN?

Your journal entries are end-to-end encrypted — scrambled before they leave your device. Only you can read them. Not even we can.

Think of it like a locked diary that only unlocks with your combination — your password + PIN together form that key.

For personal and mental health data, this level of privacy really matters. Your entries stay private, always.
