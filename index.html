🔐 Why is there a PIN?

Your journal entries are end-to-end encrypted — scrambled before they leave your device. Only you can read them. Not even we can.

Think of it like a locked diary that only unlocks with your combination — your password + PIN together form that key.

For personal and mental health data, this level of privacy really matters. Your entries stay private, always.
